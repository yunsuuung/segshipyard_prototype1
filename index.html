About Company

KLCSM

KLCSM is a member of the SM Group and has provided professional total marine solutions since its establishment in 1977, including crew and vessel management, newbuilding supervision, second-hand vessel conversion supervision, ship repair and shipyard operations, and maritime technology consulting. The company currently manages a total of 67 vessels, including 14 LNG carriers (LNGCs).

Required Environment

The shipyard must meet the following conditions.

  • Shipyard worker: Over 2,200 own employees, over 6,000 sub-employees
  • Current capability: Annual repair of over 500 vessels
  • At least 2 cranes per berth
  • At least 4 cranes per dry dock
  • No double banking
  • Shore power: 1,000 kW/h or more

Shipyard worker and current capability are based on Xinya Shipyard as reference.
